FAQ

Questions your IT and operations teams will ask

Practical answers about integration, security, and ownership.

How does MITE integrate with our IT/OT landscape?
MITE supports industrial + web protocols and exposes an open API. We can push/pull data to ERP/MES/SCADA/data lakes or custom apps. You control where data lives.
Do we need developers to change workflows?
No. With declarative logic, you describe what should happen. Operations teams can adjust rules, routes, approvals, and KPIs without long dev cycles.
Is MITE suitable for regulated industries?
Yes. You can keep audit trails, approvals, and traceability as part of execution. Reports and evidence are generated from the same governed process, not assembled manually.
What happens if we need a new mechanism or capability?
If it’s a platform-level improvement (not a narrow local-only request), we typically generalize it and deliver it quickly — so you don’t pay for “one-off” plumbing.
