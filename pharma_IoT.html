Pharma: Cold Chain Monitoring

Ensuring real-time temperature control in pharmaceutical logistics using MITE.

The Challenge

Pharmaceutical products must be stored and transported within precise temperature ranges to remain effective and safe. Manual monitoring often results in gaps, non-compliance, and product loss.

The MITE Solution

MITE digitizes the cold chain by collecting real-time data from temperature and humidity sensors placed inside storage units and delivery vehicles. It triggers alerts when thresholds are breached and automatically logs all events for full audit readiness.

Results

Clients achieved 100% regulatory compliance with EU GMP and FDA standards, reduced product losses by over 30%, and cut manual reporting time in half. The system operates 24/7 with zero human intervention.
